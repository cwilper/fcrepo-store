Using fcrepo-store-legacy

Installation

To use this library in your own Maven-based project, just add the following to your pom.xml:

    <dependency>
      <groupId>com.github.cwilper.fcrepo-store</groupId>
      <artifactId>fcrepo-store-legacy</artifactId>
      <version>0.1.0-SNAPSHOT</version>
    </dependency>

You can also download the jar and its dependencies directly from Maven Central:

  • fcrepo-store-legacy-0.1.0-SNAPSHOT.jar
  • All dependencies
